ChatGPT Invoice Download Instructions
Domains required: chatgpt.com, pay.openai.com
Steps to Access Billing & Download Invoices:

Navigate to ChatGPT: Go to https://chatgpt.com (user must be logged in)
Open Profile Menu: Click on the profile/account area in the bottom-left corner of the sidebar (shows user name and subscription type like "Plus")
Open Settings: Click "Settings" from the dropdown menu
Go to Account Tab: In the Settings dialog, click on "Account" in the left sidebar
Access Payment Management: In the Account section, find the "Payment" area and click the "Manage" button - this opens the Stripe billing portal at pay.openai.com
Find Invoice History: Scroll down on the billing page to find the "INVOICE HISTORY" section which lists all past invoices with dates, amounts, and payment status
Find the latest invoice row and get the link to it 

Notes:

The billing portal is powered by Stripe
Invoice history shows date, amount, status (Paid), and subscription type
Click "View more" to see older invoices beyond the initial display
Each invoice opens in a new tab on invoice.stripe.com
